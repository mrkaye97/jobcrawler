Hi! Welcome to Jobcrawler

Jobcrawler is a tool to send you notifications when a company you love posts a new job that matches your interests.

It's pretty simple. There's a set of companies you can select from (and you can add more!). You can create a "query" by specifying a search term (accepts regex), and then the app will send you an email if the company you selected posts a job that matches your specified query.

Feel free to email me or submit an issue on the app's Github repo with suggestions for improvements or new feature requests!

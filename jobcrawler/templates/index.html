Hi! Welcome to Jobcrawler

This is a tool for passive job searching. It will send you notifications when a company you love posts a new job that matches your interests. This way, you can keep up to date with new jobs at companies you're interested in without constantly needing to check their job boards.

If you're actively hunting, it might be better to use a more feature-complete job aggregator. But if you have a shortlist of companies you'd like to work at and want to be able to pounce on a new opportunity they post at any given time, this might just be the tool for you!

Feel free to email me or submit an issue on the app's Github repo with suggestions for improvements or new feature requests!
